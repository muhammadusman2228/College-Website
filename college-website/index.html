About Us

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Greenfield College has been a beacon of quality education since its founding in 2000. Located in the heart of the city, our institution provides a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment for students to thrive and discover their potential.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, a rich library, and a dedicated faculty of over 120 expert educators, we offer an unparalleled educational experience across multiple disciplines.

Admissions 2025

Apply in four simple steps and begin your journey toward an exceptional future.

Step 01
Online Application
Complete the online application form with your personal details, academic records, and program preference. Applications are accepted year-round.
Step 02
Submit Documents
Upload scanned copies of your CNIC, SSC and HSSC certificates, domicile, and two passport-size photographs through our secure portal.
Step 03
Assessment & Interview
Shortlisted candidates will be called for an entry test and/or interview with the concerned faculty department. Results are announced within 7 days.
Step 04
Enroll & Begin
Upon receiving your admission letter, complete fee deposit in the Student Portal, collect your student ID, and begin your academic journey!
